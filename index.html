FINISH STRONG WITH PROVEN CLINICAL BENEFITS

For acute injury and post-operative recovery, the portable GRPro® 2.1 cold and compression system is clinically proven to help reduce opioid consumption, decrease pain and swelling, and may increase satisfaction with the recovery process.

The innovative multi-modality, dual-user Med4 Elite® integrates iceless cold, heat, contrast, and compression therapies like never before to deliver flexible, streamlined, and proven treatment options for different users and diverse stages of rehabilitation.
